We provide traditional South Indian food and share our culture and stories with guests. We have wide varieties of non-vegetarian and vegetarian foods to bring the malayali out of you.

The traditional food of Kerala contains various spices that make the food savory, including cinnamon, cloves, ginger, and black pepper. The dishes usually involve rice, coconut, and tapioca. We will be bringing in more unique regional favorites on our weekend special menu.
